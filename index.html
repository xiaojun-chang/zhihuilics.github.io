Zhihui Li – Homepage

 

Greetings! Now I have been working as a research assistant with School of Computer Science and Technology at Shandong University since January 2018. I received the B.S. degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2008. After that, I have worked as a Data Analyst in Beijing Etrol Technologies Co., Ltd from July 2008 to December 2017. My research interests include artificial intelligence, machine learning, and computer vision. I have published more than 10 technical papers at prominent journals and conferences such as IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering (TKDE), IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics (TCYB), IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics (TII), Computer Vision and Image Understanding (CVIU), Neural Computation (NC), AAAI, Australasian Database Conference (ADC), etc.
